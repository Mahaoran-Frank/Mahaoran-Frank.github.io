# Frank Ma Personal Homepage

This is my personal homepage and public output layer.

The site is not meant to expose my whole Obsidian vault. My Obsidian system remains my private workspace for raw materials, unfinished thinking, project notes, and personal planning.

This website is the public layer: a curated place where selected outputs can become readable, shareable, and verifiable.

Live site: https://mahaoran-frank.github.io/

## Why This Exists

I use Obsidian as a long-term knowledge system, and AI as a collaborator that can work with my accumulated context.

This homepage is a small public surface for that workflow. It helps turn learning, reading, projects, and experiments into visible outputs over time.

The goal is not to publish everything. The goal is to make the right outputs easier to find, explain, and improve.

## How It May Grow

Future outputs may include project writeups, reading notes, AI workflow experiments, product thinking, or other public-facing artifacts.

The structure will stay lightweight at first. As the number of outputs grows, the site may evolve into a more template-driven output system.

## Tech

Static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, hosted on GitHub Pages.
